# 🚀 Under The Hood University (UTHU)

Welcome to **Under The Hood University (UTHU)**, a self-built, high-velocity, code-first learning system designed to help you master the skills needed for AI, MLOps, DevOps, Cloud, LLMs, Fullstack Engineering, and more. This is a practical, project-based learning journey focused on **real-world skills**.

---

## 📘 About UTHU
- **Objective**: Master AI, MLOps, DevOps, Cloud, LLMs, Fullstack Engineering, and more through hands-on projects and code-first learning.
- **Modules**:
  - [Python Basics](./00_software_engineering_foundations/01_python_basics)
  - [ML Projects](./03_ml_basics)
  - [Deep Learning](./04_deep_learning)
  - [MLOps & DevOps](./06_mlops)
  - [Cloud Computing](./01_data_engineering)
  - [LLMs & LangChain](./05_llm_engineering)
  - [Capstone Projects](./07_capstone_projects)
  - [Soft Skills & Ethics](./08_soft_skills)
- **Learning Resources**: Jupyter Notebooks, markdown guides, templates, and real-world projects.

## 🌍 Key Highlights
- **Real-world skills** with runnable lessons and structured prompts.
- Designed to **outperform traditional CS degrees** through practical learning.
- **Build projects** from scratch that include deployment, monitoring, and scaling.

## 🎓 UTHU vs IIT Bombay AI – Which Path Fits You?

| Category               | **Under The Hood University (UTHU)**      | **IIT Bombay AI Course**      |
|------------------------|------------------------------------------|------------------------------|
| 🎯 **Goal**             | Hands-on, build fast, industry-ready    | Strong theory, research path |
| 🛠️ **Learning Style**   | Code-first, project-driven, self-paced  | Lectures, papers, academic rigor |
| 🚀 **Focus**            | AI, MLOps, Cloud, Fullstack, DevOps     | AI theory, ML mathematics, algorithms |
| ⏳ **Duration**         | ~3 months (intensive)                   | Semester-based (~6 months+)  |
| 📦 **Outcome**          | Portfolio, deployed projects, practical skills | Deep understanding, research prep |
| 👨‍💻 **Ideal For**      | Builders, job seekers, self-learners    | Academic minds, future PhDs |

> 🔥 If your goal is **“Get skilled → Build stuff → Get hired fast,”**  
> 👉 **UTHU** will get you there.

> 🧠 If you're aiming for **“Publish papers → Pursue research → Grad school,”**  
> 🎓 **IIT Bombay’s AI course** is gold.
